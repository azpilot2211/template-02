Frequently asked questions
What services do you offer?

We offer a full range of landscaping services, including landscape design, lawn care and maintenance, hardscaping (like patios and walkways), irrigation system installation, and seasonal cleanups. Whether you need a one-time service or ongoing care, we can customize a plan that fits your needs.

How much does a landscaping project cost?

Pricing varies depending on the size and complexity of the project. We offer free consultations to discuss your goals and budget, and we’ll provide a customized quote that reflects the scope of work. Our goal is to offer high-quality service at a fair price.

Can you help me design my landscape from scratch?

Absolutely! Our team specializes in custom landscape design, working with you from concept to completion. We start with a design consultation to understand your vision, then create a detailed plan that considers factors like your style, space, and budget.

What kind of maintenance services do you provide?

Our maintenance services cover lawn mowing, edging, fertilization, weed control, pruning, and seasonal cleanups. You can opt for one-time visits or recurring services, depending on what best suits your landscape’s needs.

How long does a typical landscaping project take?

The timeline depends on the project’s scope and the services involved. Smaller projects, like installing a garden bed, may take just a few days, while more extensive projects, such as full landscape redesigns or hardscaping, could take several weeks. During our consultation, we’ll provide a clear timeline based on your specific project.

Do you offer any eco-friendly landscaping options?

Yes, we prioritize sustainable landscaping practices, including water-efficient irrigation, native plant selections, and organic fertilization options. We’re committed to creating beautiful, environmentally-friendly landscapes that conserve water and promote biodiversity.

Can I make changes to the design once the project has started?

We understand that ideas can evolve. Minor changes can often be accommodated, but major alterations may affect the timeline and budget. We’ll keep you updated throughout the project and make every effort to incorporate your preferences.

Will the work disturb my existing landscape?

We take great care to minimize disruption and protect your existing plants, lawn, and structures. Any necessary adjustments will be discussed with you, and we’ll ensure that the work area is left clean and organized each day.

How do I get started with a consultation?

It’s easy! Just reach out through our contact form, or give us a call. We’ll schedule a time to discuss your goals, offer guidance on the best solutions, and provide a detailed proposal for moving forward.
